# Kasha for one.

Kasha = porridge in Russian.

Vegan. Serves 1. Obviously.

### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup grains
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar


### Cook

1. Put things in a pot on 6.
1. Once boiling, reduce to 2 and stir vigorously until the consistency
looks ok.
