## Eating ideas

* Roast that bad boy
  * Cut in half, scoop out guts
  * In backing pan, drizzle with olive oil and set 1 cup water in the bottom
  * Bake at 160 C for 40 minutes
  * Sprinkle lightly with salt, enjoy with spoon <3
